# xtfifevd 1.0.0

* Initial CRAN release.
* Implements FEVD (Plumper & Troeger 2007) and FEF/FEF-IV (Pesaran & Zhou 2016).
* Provides Pesaran-Zhou corrected standard errors for time-invariant regressors.
* Supports comparison of corrected vs raw FEVD standard errors (diagnostic).
* Three estimation methods: FEVD, FEF, FEF-IV.
